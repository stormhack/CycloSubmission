About Us

Redefining Cycling in R-Land Formulated by four 1st yearites from ECE, Cyclo tries to integrate the cycling junta of our college with the tech savvy generation of our modern world. Cyclo is mainly focused on creating a user-friendly interface for its users and providing well maintained cycles. There has been more emphasis on implementing convenient and quick service for its patrons. Cyclo is determined to contribute to innovative solution making to simple problems which are relevant at a college wide scale.
